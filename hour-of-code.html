What is Hour of Code?

The Hour of Code is a worldwide movement that aims to introduce millions of students to computer science through hour-long programming activities. Our goal with the Hour of Code is to demystify programming and show that anyone can learn the basics, encouraging future interest in computer science.

Hour of Code Event

Video from Hour of Code in collaboration with Target High School

Before this event, we partnered with "Hedef" Lyceum to host our Hour of Code session. They generously provided us with a classroom and 20 enthusiastic children eager to learn. We kicked off the event with an inspiring video to spark their curiosity about coding. Following that, we introduced them to Programming with Angry Birds on Code.org, explaining the tasks step by step. The children then eagerly dove into the challenges, solving them with creativity and determination. It was an exciting and unique experience, filled with energy, curiosity, and a sense of accomplishment for everyone involved.
